# ConvertFiles Growth Sprint Checklist

## Goal
Build the fastest credible path toward stronger organic growth for `https://convertfiles.qzz.io` by combining new indexed content, stronger internal linking, and manual distribution work.

## Repo-side work completed
- `guides/` pages added for English image tasks, workflow problems, and Bangla demand pages.
- `compare/` pages added for common format and workflow decisions.
- `categories/` hub pages added for image, developer, text, utility, and Bangla clusters.
- tool pages strengthened with use cases, best-fit guidance, limitations, and editorial links.
- homepage, all-tools, about page, and footer updated to route traffic into guides, compare pages, and topical hubs.
- segmented sitemap files added and linked from `robots.txt`.
- analytics helpers standardized for `tool_viewed`, `tool_started`, `tool_completed`, `next_tool_click`, `share_click`, `search_click_landing`, and `organic_guide_cta_click`.
